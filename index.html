About Us

Welcome to the Platform for Budget Expenditure Monitoring,

where transparency meets technology.
We are committed to empowering citizens,
fostering accountability, and driving positive
change through innovative solutions.

What We Do

Promote Transparency: By providing real-time data on government spending, we help citizens understand how public funds are allocated and utilized.

Empower Citizens: Our platform enables users to report anomalies, monitor projects, and engage in meaningful discussions about budget expenditures.

Leverage Technology: Using advanced tools like AI-powered anomaly detection, we ensure accurate and efficient monitoring of budgetary activities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a transparent, accessible, and collaborative space where citizens, NGOs, and government agencies can work together to ensure responsible budget management. We believe in the power of technology and collective action to build trust and accountability in public governance

Current Status of Road Repair: Example from Almaty
Road: Abay Avenue (Specific Section) Location: Stretch between Nazarbayev Street and
Furmanov Street, Almaty, Kazakhstan.

Project Description:

The ongoing repair project aims to
improve road surface quality,
enhance drainage systems, and upgrade
pedestrian crossings to
ensure safety.

Status:

Repairs have been underway for 6 months but are
delayed by 2 months compared to the planned schedule.
Only 60% of the work is complete, with sections near
Furmanov Street still unpaved.

Budget:

Allocated: 500 million KZT.
Spent: 320 million KZT as per latest updates.
Anomalies: Concerns raised over high costs for asphalt and
concrete compared to market prices.

Quality:

Recently completed sections show uneven surfaces
and drainage issues during heavy rains. Residents have
complained about temporary pedestrian paths being unsafe

Positive

Acknowledgment that repairs were needed due to previous road deterioration.
Support for the inclusion of pedestrian-friendly infrastructure.

Negative

Lack of clear communication about project delays.
Concerns over potential mismanagement or misuse of funds.

Recommendation for monitoring


Transparency
Regular updates on the project’s progress and financial details on
a public platform.
Breakdowns of expenses for materials, labor, and additional costs.


Quality Control
Independent inspection of completed sections to ensure compliance with standards.


Community Engagement:
Allow residents to report issues or anomalies directly via a
dedicated reporting tool.
